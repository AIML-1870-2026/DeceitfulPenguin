Why these weights?

Wind (w = -5.0): AOPA data shows wind is the primary cause in the majority of general aviation landing accidents. Risk increases near-exponentially above 20 kts.

Stress (w = -3.0): NTSB identified pilot fatigue/stress in 4-7% of civil aviation incidents. Stress impairs decision-making and situational awareness.

Traffic (w = -2.0): FAA notes that 45% of near-midair collisions occur in airport traffic patterns within 30 miles of airports.

Price (w = -1.5): Economic consideration only — no direct safety correlation. Included to demonstrate that not all inputs carry equal weight.
