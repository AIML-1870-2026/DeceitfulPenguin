      About MedCheck

      MedCheck is a free, informational tool for looking up potential drug-drug interactions in plain language.

      Interaction information is extracted from official openFDA drug labels (FDA-approved package inserts), which document known interactions as reported to the U.S. Food & Drug Administration.

      Results are for informational purposes only. MedCheck does not provide medical advice. Always consult a licensed pharmacist or physician before making changes to your medication regimen.

      openFDA data is in the public domain (U.S. Government work).
